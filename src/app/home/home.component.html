Little Leo

Feel like home

Little Leo Homestay and Hostel is situated in Hoi An, 1.3 km from Japanese Covered Bridge. A terrace or balcony are featured in certain rooms. A flat-screen TV is featured. You will find a shared kitchen at the property. The homestay also offers free use of bicycles. Assembly Hall of the Cantonese Chinese Congregation is 1.3 km from Little Leo Homestay, while Assembly Hall of the Fujian Chinese Congregation is 1.4 km from the property. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 22 km from the property. Tan An is a great choice for travellers interested in city walks, culture and atmosphere. We speak your language!
